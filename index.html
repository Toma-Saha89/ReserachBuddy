Platform Features

Everything Researchers Need

1

Intelligent AI Matchmaking

Our AI engine analyzes research intent, expertise, and collaboration patterns using semantic embeddings — moving the industry from passive networking to smart, precision matchmaking.

2

Dedicated Collaboration Workspaces

Built-in workspaces to share datasets, track research milestones, and co-author papers in real time — turning a match into a productive working relationship immediately.

3

Verified Identity System

Anti-ghosting verification using university domain emails, ORCID iDs, GitHub, and Google Scholar — ensuring every collaborator is who they say they are.

4

AI Research Assistant (Coming Soon)

Future features include paper summarization, methodology suggestions, dataset recommendations, literature support, and automated research structuring using LLM and RAG-based technologies.
